Quick Facts

Every 2 seconds, someone needs blood.

Accident victims can require 100+ units.

Less than 10% of eligible donors give blood annually.

Blood cannot be manufactured, only donated.

One donation can save up to 3 lives.

Red cells can be donated every 56 days.

Donated red cells last up to 42 days.

O- is the universal red cell donor.

AB+ is the universal plasma donor.
